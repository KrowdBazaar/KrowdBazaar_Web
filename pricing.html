How is KrowdBazaar different from other e-commerce platforms?

KrowdBazaar can be added to your existing website and doesn’t require you to start over. Plus, KrowdBazaar has a Starter plan, not a Starter trial.

Are there setup or transaction fees?

No. There are no setup or transaction fees with any of our plans.

Can I change or cancel plans?

Yes. All plans are available month-to-month with no long term commitments so you can change your plan at any time.

Do you charge bandwidth or storage fees?

No. All KrowdBazaar plans include unlimited bandwidth and storage at no extra charge.

Can I use my own domain name or an existing site?

Absolutely. Your KrowdBazaar store can be added to an existing website residing on your own domain name.

What about security?

Your customers' payment information is completely safe at Ecwid. All sensitive information always is transmitted via secure HTTPS channel. Moreover KrowdBazaar is also a  PCI DSS validated Level 1 Service Provider which is the gold standard for  e-commerce solutions worldwide.

What currency will I be billed in?

All plans are billed in US dollars.

What payment options do you support?

KrowdBazaar supports over 40 payment providers globally, including PayPal and Stripe, so there’s a good chance we support your preferred option.

Do you offer refunds?

We don’t offer refunds, however, our Starter plan enables to you start selling on a fully functional store. You can upgrade to a premium whenever you’re ready!

Do you offer any discounts for nonprofit organizations?

Yes. Tell us more about your nonprofit and get 6 months Venture for Starter.
